Full job description
Hiring: Junior Full-Stack Developer (Next.js + FastAPI) – Part-Time / Full Time On-Site

Location: Location: Dubai, UAE - Hybrid
Duration: Part Time/ Contract Basis.
Hours: Part-Time (20 hours per week, flexible weekly schedule), Full time 40 Hours per week.
Salary: Competitive monthly retainer Salary or project-based contract rate.
Legal Requirement: Must hold your own valid UAE Visa (Spouse/Family sponsorship, Student Visa, or Freelance Visa)
Work Arrangement

This is a hybrid role.

We prefer someone who can spend time working on-site with the founder during key development phases to collaborate, review features, and make product decisions together. Remote work is absolutely fine for the remaining days, provided communication and progress remain consistent.

About Company:
We are an early-stage FinTech SaaS company (Registered in Dubai) building a modern portfolio risk management platform. The core application is already built. The founder has full technical and product clarity and will guide you step-by-step.

In this role, you will translate clear technical direction into clean, production-ready code, helping accelerate our upcoming product launch. This is an exceptional opportunity to gain hands-on experience deploying a complex financial technology application and a brilliant opportunity to get a financial SaaS product launch on your resume.

Technical Stack & Tools

Frontend: Next.js (App Router), React, TypeScript, Tailwind CSS
Backend: FastAPI (Python), PostgreSQL, SQLAlchemy
Version Control: Git / GitHub
Workflow Optimization: Advanced proficiency leveraging AI development assistants (e.g., Claude, ChatGPT) to accelerate debugging, boilerplate generation, and codebase exploration.
What You Will Do (Under Direct Founder Guidance)

Write Next.js frontend code to match dashboard mockups.
Hook up backend FastAPI endpoints to our PostgreSQL database.
Complete basic user authentication and profile management steps.
Debug existing workflows and test the application alongside the founder.
Profile We Are Looking For

Based in Dubai: Able to commute to our office regularly. Office is located 5 minutes of walk from Dubai Metro.
Technical Foundation: Demonstrated experience building and deploying functional projects utilizing Next.js and Python/FastAPI (academic or personal portfolio projects are acceptable).
Legal Visa Status: You already possess a valid UAE residency visa; we will provide the necessary part-time work permit/NOC approvals from Meydan Free Zone.
AI-Leveraged: You know how to effectively prompt and use AI tools (like Claude) to debug, write boilerplate code, optimize coding efficiency, troubleshoot errors, and speed up delivery times.
What We Offer

Zero corporate red tape—immediate decision-making.
Clean, documented codebase ready for final execution.
Opportunity to make a real impact on the product and technical direction.
Long-term growth opportunities as the company expands.
